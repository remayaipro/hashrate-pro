Best GPU for Mining in 2026: Complete Guide

Updated March 2026 | 8 min read

TL;DR: The RTX 4070 offers the best efficiency-to-price ratio. The RTX 4090 has the highest raw hashrate. AMD RX 7900 XTX is competitive for altcoins. Always use our profitability calculator before buying.

Top GPUs for Mining in 2026

1. NVIDIA RTX 4090 - Best Performance

  • Hashrate: ~130 MH/s (varies by algorithm)
  • Power: 350W
  • Price: $1,600 - $2,000
  • Daily profit: ~$1.31 (at $0.12/kWh)
  • Best for: ETC, RVN, ERG mining

2. NVIDIA RTX 4070 - Best Value

  • Hashrate: ~60 MH/s
  • Power: 220W
  • Price: $550 - $700
  • Daily profit: ~$0.44 (at $0.12/kWh)
  • Best for: Budget-conscious miners

3. AMD RX 7900 XTX - Best AMD Option

  • Hashrate: ~115 MH/s
  • Power: 355W
  • Price: $800 - $1,000
  • Daily profit: ~$1.03 (at $0.12/kWh)
  • Best for: AMD enthusiasts, altcoin mining

Key Factors When Choosing a Mining GPU

1. Hashrate per Dollar

Calculate MH/s per dollar spent. The RTX 4070 delivers 0.09 MH/s per dollar vs RTX 4090's 0.07 MH/s per dollar.

2. Power Efficiency

Lower power = higher profit. The RTX 4070 uses 220W for 60 MH/s (0.27 MH/W) vs RTX 4090's 350W for 130 MH/s (0.37 MH/W).

3. Resale Value

NVIDIA cards hold value better. RTX 4070 retains ~60% value after 2 years vs AMD's ~50%.

4. Algorithm Flexibility

Some coins favor AMD (KAS), others NVIDIA. Consider mining multiple coins to maximize profit.

Mining Profitability Tips

  1. Electricity is king: If you pay above $0.10/kWh, GPU mining may not be profitable
  2. Use NiceHash: Auto-switches to most profitable coin
  3. Undervolt: Reduce power 20-30% with minimal hashrate loss
  4. Join a pool: Solo mining is not viable for most
  5. Track costs: Include hardware depreciation in profit calculations
