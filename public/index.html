❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is crypto mining still profitable in 2026?

Yes, but margins are tight. Profitability depends on your electricity cost, hardware efficiency, and chosen coin. GPU miners with $0.08/kWh or lower power costs can still profit on altcoins.

What's the best GPU for mining?

The RTX 4070 offers the best efficiency-to-price ratio. For maximum hashrate, the RTX 4090 leads but has higher upfront cost. AMD RX 7900 XTX is competitive for certain algorithms.

How do I start mining?

1) Choose your hardware. 2) Download mining software (NiceHash QuickMiner for beginners). 3) Create a wallet. 4) Join a pool. 5) Start earning. Our calculator helps you pick the right coin.

What about electricity costs?

Electricity is 60-80% of mining costs. Miners paying above $0.10/kWh should focus on the most efficient coins. Consider solar or off-peak rates to improve margins.
